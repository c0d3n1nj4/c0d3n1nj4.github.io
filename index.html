About Me


There's a difference between "Love what you do" and "Do what you love" and I'm doing the latter.

I'm a Web Master for more than 5 years with some experience as a Project Manager. I deal mostly on PHP, MySQL, XHTML, CSS, AJAX, JQuery and MVC platforms (Laravel, CodeIgniter, CakePHP and Zend). I am also a WordPress and Shopify lover. As a Project Manager, I lead the planning and implementation of project. I work closely with the fulfillment team to make sure projects are in schedule and in the right direction to achieve quality project outputs. With my leadership, organizational, analytical and technical skills, I’m sure all projects will be handled efficiently.

As a Web Master I do the coding of the design and setup the site on the server. Communication is the key for a successful launch of the website. Starting from communicating with the client and with the designer.

Regarding Wordpress, I usually tell my clients to choose the template that they like the most from any online market like Envato. I sometimes help them pick the options and choose from there. I am not a designer (but I can manage Photoshop for small graphic changes) so I have the designer do his thing and have it approved. Once all designs were approved I'll take over and customize the design to fit to the template that they bought. If only small changes are needed on the template that was bought then I'll go ahead and customize it directly. I usually look for existing plugins that we can use and customize it and if we can't find anything then I'll create the plugin myself. I'll do a lot of tests including mobile responsiveness before deploying live.

On top of that, I’ve also work as a Call Center Representative with SSI (formerly Western Wats/Opinionology) for more 2 years so communication or language barrier is not a hindrance.
